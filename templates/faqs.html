FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! All keystrokes are stored securely in an encrypted database. You can also enable additional encryption for your logged data, and only you have access to your devices' information.

Which operating systems are supported?

Currently, our keylogger supports both macOS and Windows operating systems. Each platform has its own optimized implementation for maximum reliability and performance.

How do I start monitoring a device?

Simply click the "New Connection" button in the dashboard, select your operating system, and follow the setup instructions. Once connected, you can start/stop logging and view the collected data at any time.

Do I need special permissions?

Yes. On macOS, you'll need to grant Accessibility permissions in System Preferences. On Windows, you may need to run the application with administrator privileges. These permissions are required for the keylogger to function properly.

How often is the data updated?

Keystrokes are collected in real-time and saved to the database every few seconds. The device status is updated every 30 seconds, and you can manually refresh the dashboard at any time.

Can I delete my logged data?

Yes! You can remove any device from your dashboard, which will automatically delete all associated keystroke data. Your privacy is important to us, and you have full control over your data.

Can I monitor multiple devices?

Yes, you can monitor multiple devices simultaneously. Each device will appear in your dashboard with its own controls and log history. You can easily switch between devices and manage them independently.

Can I export the logged data?

Currently, you can view all logged keystrokes directly in the dashboard. We're working on adding export functionality to allow downloading logs in various formats for further analysis.

Will I be notified of device status changes?

The dashboard shows real-time status updates for all your devices. You can see when a device is active, logging, or disconnected. We're working on adding email notifications for important status changes.
